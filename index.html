Parallax is a digital agency in Leeds and London specialising in marketing and innovation. We make the web work.

Hello. Parallax is a digital agency in Leeds and London. We design and build websites and apps, write engaging content, make bespoke software, get friendly with search engines and turn our hands to all kinds of design, development and digital marketing.
As an award-winning digital agency in Leeds and London, our creative process revolves entirely around your needs. From digital products and platforms through to bespoke web solutions and services, we’ll revolutionise the way you do business. We approach every project – big or small – with the same level of thought and care. Bring us an idea, and we’ll bring it to life. We’re one of the fastest growing tech companies in the north of England – officially. And as a key partner of both Amazon Web Services and Serverless, we talk regularly at major digital events. Our team features some of the most talented minds in the industry. We work hard to stay on top of the newest innovations and always strive to be the very best at what we do. Having been established in 2010, we’ve been around long enough to cultivate an internal culture focussed around taking pride in our work, going the extra mile for clients and buying into taking on the challenges that digital presents to our customers. We have a team that’s focussed around quality, not quantity, with experts in project management, design, development and marketing that are used to working together as a cohesive team in an environment where the only benchmark that matters is customer success.
